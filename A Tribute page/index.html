Baba Maharaj Satarkar

1936-2023

The Magician of Music

Baba Maharaj Satarkar-the man who cast magic with kirtans, Satarkar's original name was Neelkanth Dnyaneshwar Gore,Born on February 5, 1936, in Satara to Gore Satarkar family, Satarkar studied law. He then carried on the 135-year legacy of the Varkari community to which his family belongs, taking up kirtan and preaching handed down to him by three generations. Satarkar family is ranked among the highest kirtankars of the Varkari community. Educated in English medium till standard 10, he used Kirtan for social reforms to bring about changes in society. He gained knowledge from his cousins Appa Maharaj and Anna Maharaj in altruism. From the age of 11 years, he took lessons in classical music from Purohit Buva and Latafat Hussain Khan of the Agra family. Baba Maharaj Satarkar, a prominent figure in Maharashtra who helped the masses understand the teachings of Lord Vitthal, passed away at the age of 87. Born into a family of worshippers of Lord Vitthal, Satarkar had the ability to articulate the writings of saints Dnyaneshwar and Tukaram. He popularized Kirtan, Pravachan, and Nirupan, and his teachings inspired hundreds of youths to turn towards these practices. Satarkar's discourses focused on the idea that life is a celebration and emphasized the importance of both God and life. His Kirtan music cassettes sold over ten lakh copies. Prime Minister Narendra Modi expressed his condolences and praised Satarkar's impact on society.
